The Poisoned Crown

The Poisoned Crown is an upcoming fantasy adventure webtoon about a fiesty snake lady who swims to the land to assasinate a kind human king. Things get complicated when she uncovers the truth.
This story will take place in Ancient India, allowing me to showcase all the interesting parts of that world. In addition, I will get the opportunity to make beautiful traditional Indian clothing and it will feature a badass female lead. Everything I've wanted a story to have in one.
The story is something that has been in my mind since Highschool (about 5-8 years ago). I started off writing it as a written novel on Wattpad. However, the story kept evolving and I took a break. In that break I fell in love with webtoons and gave it a go with my slice of life comedy webtoon. Now that I feel confident I can illustrate this story, I will start working on it December 2020. It will be an year and a half long journey to put it out to the world. The steps to making this story include:

  1. Story Outlining and Structure
  2. Script Writing and Editing
  3. Art Style
  4. Character Designs
  5. Webtoon Design (header, footer, fonts, speech bubble style)
  6. Drawing
  7. Publishing and Marketing
