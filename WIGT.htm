Weird Indian Girl

Weird Indian Girl Tales

Weird Indian Girl Tales is a slice of life and comedy webtoon based on funny instances in my life. The webtoon had been in my mind for 1 year before I officially published it. I wanted to create something I could make quickly to get started with making comics (before starting something big like a fantasy webtoon). I had started working on this webtoon previously but then had stopped when school started. In quarantine I got the time to finally work on it and publish it on webtoons. I had low expectations since I had never been an expert in marketing but was surprised by the love and support I got. Thank you!

Episodes Explained

Episode Description Inspiration
1 Roshu is excited to see an American in India, but is excited to see an Indian in America. I was inspired by how I like to find people who are odd like me, a little Indian and a little American.
2 Golu, Roshu's sister asks her if she looks better with lipstick or not, and if she looked better with long hair or the short hair she has now. Roshu is stumped. This was inspired by my sister and me. Whenever she asks what looks better on her I don't know what to say because most of the time it looks the same to me.
3 Roshu is on LinkedIn and sees someone she knows. When LinkedIn tells her that they have viewed her profile she can't help but view their profile as well. This episode featured a cameo of one of my design friends in college. I have always clicked to see people's profiles out of curiosity which leads to both of us repeatedly viewing each other's LinkedIn profiles.
4 Roshu visits India and random strangers and her relatives interrogate her about marriage. This was based on something that had happened to my sister when we visited India.
5 This episode is a Halloween Special where Golu and Roshu, sisters, dress up as Aladdin and Jasmine to visit the genie in the Cave of Wonders. They then ask for their wishes. This story was inspired by my spiritual life, and learning that wealth and material goods are not happiness. I dressed them up as Aladdin and Jasmine because I love their Arabic costumes, which are somewhat similar to Indian clothes.
6 Roshu is in a design class where the teacher introduces the head of the art and design department. Roshu feels nervous when she is the only one who doesn't drink soda. This is based on something that happened to me in college but has a humurous twist to it.
