About Me

I am a design student in day and comic artist by night. I enjoy creating stories that show Indian culture and also share my experiences through comics. I love expressing myself through my art and love making others crack a smile or feel excited from my work. I love standing out from a crowd by my utter oddity and weirdness. I am Weird Indian Girl (don't call me wig please hahaha).
Inspiration from my comics comes from various sources. I love the art in Wrath and the Dawn, Not Even Bones, Shining Hel, etc. I also draw inspiration for lighting and colors from movies. I draw inspiration for stories from Young Adult novels, Korean Dramas, Bollywood movies, and more. I used to use Photoshop and Procreate (Ipad app) for creating my comics but recently switched over to Clip Studio Paint which has more personalized tools for specifically creating comics. A typical workflow for my work includes writing the script, thumbnailing, finding or cretaing references from 3D models, lineart, lettering, then coloring. Then I publish the comic and share it on social media.
